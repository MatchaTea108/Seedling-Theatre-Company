Semester Sibling Discount

Families who enroll more than one child in semester monthly tuition-based classes concurrently at our studio receive a 15% discount on all tuition payments . Sibling discount does not apply to production fees and does not apply if another discount offer is already being used. Use coupon code FAMILY15 when checking out online. Semester Sibling discount does not apply to Summer Camps.
