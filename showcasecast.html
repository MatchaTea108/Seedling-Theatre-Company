Fall 2022 Showcase Cast: Newsies The Musical

What Is Showcase Cast?

All students age 10-18 years who have not yet graduated from high school are welcome to audition this semester for the premier cast of the upcoming musical production at Seedling Theatre Company. Each semester will have a different cast that will become this advanced level course. Auditions will be held approximately 6 weeks prior to the start of the Fall and Spring semesters (mid-November and mid-July) Students will be asked to sing solo segments from the musical and prepare a memorized monologue or scene provided by the directors. A certain number of non-singing parts may be available. Previous performing experience is not required to audition for this cast. However, this class will focus almost exclusively on rehearsals and students will be expected to know basic stage directions, theatre terminology and etiquette. Cast members will be expected to make attending all classes and extra rehearsals a priority and show a high level of maturity, dedication, and hard work.

How To Audition

Spring 2021 Auditions for SHREK the Musical Jr. will be video auditions only. Prepare (memorized) the provided monologue for each character and a solo vocal selection from the Shrek musical assigned for that character. Music tracks for the vocal selections can be found on our SLACK app channel for Shrek auditions. Solos must be performed with the backing track only. To get connected to that channel, Click Here and submit a request.
Take a video of your audition and a) post it on a viewing platform (i.e. YouTube, Google) and submit a link OR b) upload your video through a direct message on SLACK to Ms Kris for Teens or Ms Maddie for Jrs.
Students may submit auditions for up to 3 different characters.
Students must also print out, complete, and have a parent sign the AUDITION FORMS. Scan or take a picture of your completed paperwork and send it to Ms Kris or bring it to the studio when you come for classes.
Submissions are due by Saturday, Nov. 14th!
